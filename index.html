Who I Am

About Me

Hi! I'm Miguel — a Game Development Engineer and Software Engineer who loves building anything related to games, interactive experiences, simulations, and 3D graphics engines. I've always been creative and deeply curious about how things work, which eventually led me to dedicate myself to creating software and games — and I genuinely love it. I'm the kind of person who stays up until 3am programming a chicken-shrimp desktop pet just because it's fun, constantly learning so I can bring any idea to life.

Over the years I've worn many hats: starting as a solo indie developer, then becoming a game dev professor teaching university students I genuinely grew attached to, then leading engineering teams and co-directing award-winning game development, and also building bio-simulations, AI agent swarms, and clinical research systems for cancer research.

At my core, I'm a Game Development Engineer and Technical Lead who loves bridging creative mechanics with robust technical engineering. I'm deeply obsessed with gamefeel — the invisible art of adding 'juice' to gameplay, from frame-perfect inputs and heavy hit-stops to expressive wobbly physics and screenshakes that make simply moving on screen feel satisfying.
